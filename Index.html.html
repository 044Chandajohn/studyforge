Chanda C. John is an economist, researcher, published author and social entrepreneur from Lusaka, Zambia. A graduate of the Information and Communications University (ICU) Lusaka with a Bachelor of Science in Economics and Finance, he has spent over six years at the intersection of data, policy, and human development — always asking one question: why do so many people suffer from things that could simply have been taught?

Ubuntu Global Learning Network is his answer.
